About Us

HackClan is a multiplayer game designed to teach users about cybersecurity through real-world scenarios and challenges. Players create an account, build up their skills and knowledge, and compete against other players to see who can better secure their digital assets. Users can also join and form clans to work together and climb the ranks.

  • Game World: The game takes place in a virtual world, consisting of various levels with different environments and challenges. Each level represents a different cybersecurity scenario, such as malware attacks, phishing scams, or network intrusions.
  • Characters: Players create their own avatars, which represent them in the game world. Avatars can be customized with different clothing and accessories.

HackClan is an exciting and educational cybersecurity teaching platform that combines real-world scenarios with engaging gameplay mechanics. With its focus on best practices and real tools and techniques, players will learn valuable skills that they can apply in their personal and professional lives.
